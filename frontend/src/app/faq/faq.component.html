What is Shufflepik?

Shufflepik is a website and a Discord bot.Upload images or memes on the Shufflepik site and share a randomized image with the Shufflepik bot using the /shufflepik command on Discord.

How to upload images?

You must have a Shufflepik account. After creating a shufflepik account you follow 3 easy steps (in no particular order):

  1. If none of the server's you're a member of has the Shufflepik bot, invite Shufflepik Bot to one of your servers
  2. Connect Shufflepik to your Discord account
  3. Confirm/Validate Email (sent to email used to create acct.)

After this follow the upload image prompt and you'll be able to upload images.

What do you mean "randomized" image?

When you upload an image you will be prompted to upload to a corresponding "server pool". Any user from that server can upload images to the shared server pool on Shufflepik. The Shufflepik bot selects an image at random when a user uses the /shufflepik command. It's sort of a routlette of images.

Does a Discord server share a Shufflepik account?

No. You will only see the images you upload on your Shufflepik account. You will only see another user's images when they are displayed through the /shufflepik command on Discord.

Compatible image extensions?

We are only able to process .png, .jpg, .jpeg formats. We're working on gif compatibility, should be out soon.

What happens to my images if I am kicked, banned, or leave a server?

The respective images and albums for the server(s) you are no longer a part of will be deleted.

I no longer see the album(s) for (a) particular server(s)?

You may no longer be part of that server. We delete albums that correspond to servers users are no longer a part of. If you wish to keep an album, remain part of its respective server.

If I am no longer part of a server will my images continue to be displayed by the /shufflepik command?

No, this is why albums are deleted once a user disassociates with a server. If you don't have an album for an associated server then any images previously associated with said server will be deleted.

I joined a server but it is not added to my Shufflepik 'Upload to server pool(s)'.

It takes a couple of minutes for changes to take effect. If you need a more immediate change, perform a hard page refresh on home page or navigate away from home page and back or log out and log back into your account.

Changes are not being reflected.

It take a couple of minutes for changes to take effect. If you need a more immediate change, perform a hard page refresh on home page or navigate away from home page and back or log out and log back into your account.

I think or know that someone mave have or has access to my account.

You should reset your password immediately, this is the easiest way to safeguard your account. You can reset your password using the "forgot password" option on the Shufflepik login page (you may have to log out if you're logged in to your account). A good pro tip is to always log out of your account when you're done using Shufflepik for a session. If you need anything else please contact us.
